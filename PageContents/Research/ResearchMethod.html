Research Method

Research Method:

Interview


Interview Type - Semi-structured interviews conducted through:

  • Zoom
  • In-person
  • Phone call

What did we want to learn from our interviewees? What kind of questions did we ask?

  • We want to learn about the interviewees’ experiences in san francisco and what they thought about the city
  • We ask SF resident-specific questions, international college student-specific questions, sf non-resident-specific questions, real estate investor-specific questions

People who we interviewd and reasons why we interviewed them:

# People Reason
1 A college student In order to know the student’s feelings on their experiences in San Francisco.
2 Another college student In order to know their experience living in a smaller crime frequent area of town.
3 A person that is both an SF resident and an international student In order to know the insights of their experience living in san francisco as an international student.
4 An individual who is relocating to San Francisco In order to see the insights and input on how/what people use to research the neighborhood when they move and their values, priorities, needs and pain points.
5 A real estate investor In order to learn about the strategies of investing in San Francisco.
6 Another college student relocating from out of San Francisco In order to know the interaction from them towards their community and opinion of their own definition of identity of their community surrounding them.
