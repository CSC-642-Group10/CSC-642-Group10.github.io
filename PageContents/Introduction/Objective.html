Objective


This lack of an emotional and expressive connection is the core issue that the Waves mobile application solves. By providing a community-based social platform, we are enabling users to connect their passions and interests to form new connections and communities in a way that is easy and reminiscent of other applications that users are already used to. Our goal is to link these virtual interactions to face-to-face interactions to build a more emotional and expressive experience that isn’t normally prioritized in today’s society.

We have future plans to develop more ways to expand on these new connections being formed and be able to link them to possible face-to-face interactions. Currently, we have built events and posts to fulfill this relationship between virtual and face-to-face connections and plan on possibly expanding toward unique ways of making each user a sense of identity to help form deeper connections.
