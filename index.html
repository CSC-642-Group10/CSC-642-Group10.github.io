Pitch Statement




In today’s society, people have become more reliant on virtual applications to not only build new connections but as a means of interaction. As people become more reliant on these online applications, feelings of detachment and isolation can start to build, as virtual connections lack the emotional and expressive experience that face-to-face connections have. Waves is a mobile application that enables people to connect their passions and interests to form new connections and communities in a way that is easy and reminiscent of other applications that users are already used to.

Within Waves, individuals will have the ability to find new passions and interests through our swiping feature that quickly and easily finds new communities and events to join. Our goal is to link these virtual interactions to face-to-face interactions to build a more emotional and expressive experience that isn’t normally prioritized in today’s society. Our team consists of five university students that have varying levels of mastery in the process of designing and maintaining a community-based social platform. Currently, we have built events and posts to fulfill this relationship between virtual and face-to-face connections and plan on possibly expanding toward unique ways of making each user a sense of identity to help form deeper connections.
